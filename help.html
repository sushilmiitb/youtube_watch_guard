Help & Documentation

This page provides help and documentation for the YouTube Watch Guard extension. Here you can find information about how to use the extension, manage exclusion topics, configure filtering, and more.

  • Exclusion Topics: Add topics you want to filter out from YouTube recommendations.
  • Video Actions: Choose whether to hide or delete filtered videos.
  • Sensitivity: Adjust how strictly topics are matched.
  • Shorts Removal: Optionally remove YouTube Shorts sections.
  • Not Interested: Mark videos as not interested to train YouTube's algorithm.
