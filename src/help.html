YouTube Watch Guard helps you take control of your YouTube recommendations by filtering out videos and topics you want to avoid. The extension uses semantic filtering and customizable options to make your YouTube experience more conscious and distraction-free.

Hide unwanted videos

  • Exclusion list: Add topics (e.g., "cricket", "bollywood") you want to filter out from YouTube recommendations. Videos related to these topics will be hidden or deleted based on your settings.
  • Edit / Remove: Use the pencil icon to edit a topic, or the red minus icon to remove it from your exclusion list.
  • Add: Enter a topic and click "Add" to include it in your exclusion list.
  • Done: Click to exit edit mode and return to the compact view.
When a video matches an excluded topic:
  • Deactivate: The video will be deactivated from your feed, but still present in the DOM (can be activated if needed).
  • Delete: The video will be completely removed from the page.

Hide Shorts from feed

Enable this option to remove YouTube Shorts sections from your homepage and recommendations.

Mark first 10 videos as not interested

  • On the YouTube home feed, you can mark videos as "Not Interested" to signal to YouTube's algorithm that you want to see less of that type of content. However, it's easy to fall back into unconscious viewing habits, and YouTube may start showing similar content again over time.
  • This tool automates the process: when you click Mark now, it will automatically mark the top 10 videos on your YouTube homepage as "Not Interested." This helps reinforce your conscious choices and trains the algorithm to reduce unwanted recommendations.
  • YouTube categorizes the videos it shows in your home feed. You can decide which topics you don't want to see, and use this tool to regularly mark those videos as not interested. The tool is designed to remind you to repeat this exercise for best results.
  • Note: This action cannot be undone and may affect your future recommendations.
